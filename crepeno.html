Menu Project
Bas Pasta Menu – Crepeno
This project involved designing and developing a full menu for Bas Pasta at Crepeno.

My goal was to blend Italian tradition with contemporary style, creating a menu experience that feels authentic yet modern.

I worked closely with the client to choose the color palette, typography, and layout that reflects the brand’s gourmet identity. Every page, dish photo, and graphic element was curated to convey warmth, quality, and creativity.

The deliverables included:
• Logo and brand elements
• Full food and drinks menu
• Social media visuals
• Promotional posters
